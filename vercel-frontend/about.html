ANGLE ON CANTON

Browse prediction markets with AI scores. Track paper predictions. Built for Canton blockchain.

What This Does

Browse live markets from Polymarket. See AI scores for each market. Record paper predictions to track your accuracy.

  • Browse Markets - Live data with real-time prices
  • Quality Scores - We rate each market on volume, spread, activity
  • AI Insights - Plain-language analysis of what the data shows
  • Track Predictions - Record and monitor your picks

Demo: This is a UX demo. Predictions are paper only—no real money. We're testing the product before building the full version.
