About

THE PITCH

Prediction markets work. They beat polls, pundits, and most forecasting models. But today's platforms can't serve institutions—no privacy, no compliance, no regulatory clarity.

We're building the first institutional-grade prediction market on Canton. Private transactions. Regulatory compliance. Real settlement. The same accuracy, now accessible to funds, corporates, and regulated entities.

Why This Matters

Polymarket proved demand: billions in volume, mainstream attention, real predictive power. But it's retail-only. Institutions can't touch it—compliance risk, no privacy, unclear legal status.

Canton changes that. Built by Digital Asset for institutional finance, it's already live with major banks and exchanges. DAML smart contracts give us privacy by default, atomic settlement, and a clear path to regulatory approval.

We're not competing with Polymarket. We're building what comes next: prediction markets for the institutional world.

Why Canton

Privacy: Transactions visible only to counterparties. No public ledger exposure. Institutions can trade without revealing positions.

Compliance: DAML contracts encode regulatory rules. KYC/AML built in. Audit trails for regulators without sacrificing privacy.

Interoperability: Canton connects to existing financial infrastructure. Settlement in real currencies. Integration with custody and prime brokerage.

Credibility: Digital Asset's network already runs with Goldman, BNP Paribas, Broadridge. This isn't experimental—it's production infrastructure.

The Opportunity

Prediction markets are a $1B+ category growing fast. But the institutional segment—hedge funds, corporates, governments—is completely unserved. They need prediction market accuracy but can't use existing platforms.

We're building the bridge: retail-proven mechanics + institutional-grade infrastructure.
