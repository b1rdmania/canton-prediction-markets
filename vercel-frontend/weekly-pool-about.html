About

CANTON MARKET
OF THE WEEK

One binary question. One community pool. One resolution. Canton validators can see DAML + USDC.x working in real time.

The concept

Each week the community votes on a binary question. Everyone stakes USDC.x on Yes or No. When the market resolves, winners split the pool. The entire flow executes on Canton through DAML contracts.

How it works

Vote. The community picks the Market of the Week from curated candidates.

Stake. Participants choose Yes or No and enter their USDC.x amount. Stakes feed one shared pool.

Resolve. The resolver executes the DAML contract on Canton. Winners receive payouts in USDC.x.

Repeat. The resolved market stays visible while the next vote opens.

What makes it different

No orderbook. No multi-market dashboard. No wallet friction. Just one clean ritual that shows Canton's ledger working every week.
