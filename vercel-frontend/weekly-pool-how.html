How It Works

CANTON MARKET OF THE WEEK

Weekly ritual

Each week the community votes on one binary question from curated candidates. The winner becomes the Canton Market of the Week. This cadence keeps attention focused and the ledger feels alive.

Stake into the pool

Participants pick Yes or No and enter their USDC.x stake. All stakes feed one shared pool. The market mood bar shows the Yes/No split and updates live. The split determines each side's payout multiplier.

Resolution

At the scheduled time, the resolver executes the DAML contract on Canton. The contract settles, pays winners in USDC.x, and emits a resolution event. The UI updates instantly with payouts, pool size, and timestamp.

Recap and repeat

The resolved market stays visible with full details while the next week's vote opens. Participants can see the outcome, pool size, and payout timing. This turns the ledger receipt into a readable story.

What this is not

Not an orderbook. Not a multi-market dashboard. Not a trading terminal. No wallet onboarding. No auth gates. No AI hype. Just one clean weekly ritual showing Canton's DAML + USDC.x at work.
