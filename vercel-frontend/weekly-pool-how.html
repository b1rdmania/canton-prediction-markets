How It Works

CANTON MARKET OF THE WEEK

1. Market of the Week ritual
  • Each week one curated binary question (Yes/No). Community votes from 3–5 candidates; winner becomes "Canton Market of the Week."
  • Hero area shows live question, countdown to resolution, and note: "Deliberately minimal. Built to show real on-ledger flow."
  • Concentrated cadence keeps attention and makes the ledger feel alive.
2. Stake into the parimutuel pool
  • Participants select Yes or No, enter USDC.x amount, and hit "Stake now." Stakes feed a single shared pot.
  • "Market mood" bar shows Yes/No split and updates as stakes arrive. Percentages translate to payout per share.

Terminology: participants, stakes, pools, resolution—no "bets," "orderbooks," or "gimmicks."

3. Resolution moment
  • At scheduled time designated resolver executes the DAML contract on Canton. Contract flips state, drains pot, pays winners in USDC.x.
  • UI updates instantly: status flips to "resolved," payouts display, recap card appears with question, outcome, pool size, payout, timestamp.
  • Footer line reads "Resolved on Canton · DAML execution complete" to make ledger event explicit.
4. Post-resolution recap
  • Recap card stays visible while next week's vote opens. Shows participants, pool size, outcome, payout timing.
  • Compact "Participants snapshot" highlights top 5 stakes (anonymized), signaling real people moving funds.
  • Ledger receipt turned into a story.
5. What this is not
  • Not an orderbook or multi-market dashboard.
  • Not a trading terminal with endless instruments.
  • No wallet onboarding, no auth gating, no AI hype.
  • One clean weekly ritual showing Canton's DAML + USDC.x heartbeat.
Built for Canton. One market at a time.
