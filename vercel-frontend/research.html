HOW WE SCORE MARKETS

We score each market on volume, spread, activity, and time to resolution. Higher scores mean better data.

Quality Scores

Not all markets are equal. Some have thin volume, wide spreads, or few traders. We score each market so you can focus on the ones with the best data.

Volume Score

Based on 24-hour trading volume. More volume means better price discovery.

Spread Score

How tight is the bid-ask spread? Tighter spreads mean lower trading costs.

Activity Score

Number of trades and unique traders. More activity suggests broader consensus.

Time Score

Time until resolution. Markets near resolution often have better prices.
