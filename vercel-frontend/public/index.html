Our quality score (0-100) combines four factors: Liquidity (35 pts), Spread tightness (25 pts), Trading activity (25 pts), and Time to resolution (15 pts). Higher scores indicate more reliable markets with better price discovery.

We build on 40+ years of academic research including Berg & Rietz (2018) on longshots and overconfidence, Munger's 25 cognitive biases, and proven mean reversion strategies that are currently running live on Polymarket with real capital.
