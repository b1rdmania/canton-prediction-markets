About Canton Prediction Markets

An AI-enhanced prediction markets platform built on the Canton blockchain, combining the power of DAML smart contracts, real-world market data, and cutting-edge AI analysis to create a transparent, privacy-preserving prediction market ecosystem.

Mission: To democratize access to prediction markets while leveraging AI and blockchain technology to provide unparalleled insights and security for participants.

Canton Prediction Markets brings together three powerful technologies:

  • Canton Blockchain - Privacy-preserving, Swiss-compliant distributed ledger
  • Real-Time Market Data - Aggregated from Kalshi and Polymarket
  • AI-Powered Insights - Grok API with RAG-enhanced analysis

üèóÔ∏è Architecture

Smart Contracts (DAML)

All predictions are executed through DAML smart contracts deployed on the Canton network. This ensures:

  • Immutable record of all predictions
  • Automatic resolution based on real-world outcomes
  • Privacy-preserving transactions
  • Swiss regulatory compliance

AI Analysis Engine

Our AI system uses Grok (xAI) to provide intelligent insights:

  • Market Summaries - Concise explanations of each prediction event
  • Probability Estimates - AI-assisted likelihood calculations
  • Historical Context (RAG) - Retrieves similar past events from vector database
  • Confidence Scoring - Evaluates data quality and consistency
