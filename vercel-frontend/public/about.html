About

CANTON PREDICTION MARKETS.

An AI-enhanced prediction markets platform built on the Canton blockchain, combining the power of DAML smart contracts, real-world market data, and cutting-edge AI analysis to create a transparent, privacy-preserving prediction market ecosystem.

Mission

To democratize access to prediction markets while leveraging AI and blockchain technology to provide unparalleled insights and security for participants.

Overview

WHAT WE DO.

Canton Prediction Markets brings together three powerful technologies:

  • Canton Blockchain - Privacy-preserving, Swiss-compliant distributed ledger
  • Real-Time Market Data - Aggregated from Kalshi and Polymarket
  • AI-Powered Insights - Research-backed analysis with RAG-enhanced context
Architecture

HOW IT'S BUILT.

Smart Contracts (DAML)

All predictions are executed through DAML smart contracts deployed on the Canton network:

  • Immutable record of all predictions
  • Automatic resolution based on real-world outcomes
  • Privacy-preserving transactions
  • Swiss regulatory compliance

AI Analysis Engine

Our AI system provides intelligent insights backed by 40+ years of research:

  • Market Summaries - Concise explanations of each prediction event
  • Probability Estimates - AI-assisted likelihood calculations
  • Historical Context (RAG) - Retrieves similar past events from vector database
  • Confidence Scoring - Evaluates data quality and consistency

PRIVACY & COMPLIANCE.

Privacy-Preserving Architecture

  • Confidential transactions with selective disclosure
  • No private keys stored on servers
  • Swiss data protection standards
  • Auditable yet private prediction history

Swiss Compliance

Canton Prediction Markets is designed with Swiss regulatory framework in mind:

  • Data sovereignty (Swiss-based infrastructure)
  • FINMA-aware design patterns
  • Privacy by design (GDPR compliant)
  • Transparent governance model
