About

AM PREDICTS MVP.

A demo platform for browsing prediction markets with AI-powered insights and quality scoring.

What This Demo Does

Am Predicts MVP lets you browse live prediction markets from Polymarket, see AI-generated insights with quality scores, and record paper predictions to track your forecasting accuracy.

  • Browse Markets - Live data from Polymarket with real-time probabilities
  • Quality Scores - Algorithm-based scoring to identify reliable markets
  • AI Insights - Plain-language analysis of market conditions
  • Track Predictions - Record and monitor your paper predictions

Demo Purpose: This is a UX demo for stakeholder review. Predictions are paper/mock only - no real funds or on-chain settlement. The goal is to validate the product direction and user experience.

Future Vision

The full version will include Canton blockchain integration with DAML smart contracts for privacy-preserving, on-chain prediction settlement. This demo validates the UX before building the complete system.

  • Phase 1 (Current) - Demo MVP with paper predictions
  • Phase 2 - Canton/DAML integration for on-chain settlement
  • Phase 3 - Advanced AI insights and portfolio tracking
