Our Approach

HOW WE SCORE MARKETS.

We combine live market data with quality scoring algorithms to help you identify the most reliable prediction markets.

Quality Scoring

Not all prediction markets are created equal. Some have thin liquidity, wide spreads, or low participation. Our quality scoring system evaluates each market on multiple dimensions to help you focus on markets with the best data.

Liquidity Score

Based on 24-hour trading volume. Higher volume means more reliable price discovery and easier entry/exit.

Spread Score

Measures the bid-ask spread tightness. Tighter spreads indicate more efficient markets with lower trading costs.

Activity Score

Tracks number of trades and unique participants. More activity suggests broader consensus on pricing.

Time Score

Considers time until resolution. Markets closer to resolution often have more accurate pricing.

AI-Generated Insights

Our AI analyzes each market's metrics and generates plain-language insights to help you understand what the data suggests. These insights combine the quality scores with market probability to give you a quick read on each opportunity.

The insights highlight factors like market consensus strength, data quality, and potential considerations - all designed to inform your prediction decisions.

Note: All insights are heuristic and experimental. They're designed to surface interesting patterns in the data, not to provide financial advice. Always do your own research before placing predictions.
