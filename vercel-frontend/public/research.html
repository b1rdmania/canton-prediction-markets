üîó How Research Powers Canton AI

Quality Scoring Algorithm

The same 100-point quality scoring system used by trading models now powers AI confidence levels:

  • Liquidity Score (0-35): Based on 24h volume
  • Spread Score (0-25): Bid-ask spread tightness
  • Activity Score (0-25): Number of trades and participants
  • Time Score (0-15): Time to resolution

Mean Reversion Detection

AI insights flag when current probabilities deviate significantly from:

  • Historical averages for similar events
  • Fundamental probability estimates
  • Cross-market consistency checks

Behavioral Bias Detection

AI recognizes common biases that create mispricing:

  • Longshot bias (overpricing unlikely events)
  • Recency bias (overweighting recent news)
  • Confirmation bias (selective information processing)
